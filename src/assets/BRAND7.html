Welcome to Rehman Twin Tower

Saif Residor Nathia Gali is a premium 4-star serviced apartment project by H-MAK Pvt Ltd., ideally located in the lush green surroundings of Main Nathia Gali. The project features fully furnished studio and 1-bedroom luxury apartments, with world-class hotel services and modern amenities — all available with a 30% down payment and 48-month flexible installment plan.

About the Project

Saif Residor is designed to be a serene mountain escape — offering comfort, luxury, and natural harmony. These hotel-serviced apartments blend contemporary living with the charm of the Himalayan landscape. The development includes:

  • Studio & 1-Bed Fully Furnished Apartments

  • Commercial Shops and Business Units

  • Hotel-Style Services and Amenities

  • Smart Eco-Friendly Infrastructure

Each unit offers breathtaking balcony views, cozy fireplaces, fully equipped kitchens, and connectivity through high-speed Wi-Fi. The entire project promotes eco-conscious living through solar energy usage, rainwater harvesting, and waste reduction initiatives.
